Jay Gatsby

Jay Gatsby is a wealthy and mysterious man with an intense charisma, known for his extravagant parties in West Egg, New York. These parties served the main purpose of winning back, Daisy Buchanan, the love of his life. Despite his success, Gatsby is isolated, as his wealth cannot buy him the acceptance or affection he truly craves. Ultimately, his idealism and fixation on the past lead to his tragic downfall, revealing the emptiness behind the American Dream he tried so hard to attain.
